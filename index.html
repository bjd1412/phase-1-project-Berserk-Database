h1>What is Berserk?

"Berserk" (ベルセルク Beruseruku) is a renowned dark fantasy manga series crafted by the talented Kentaro Miura.
The genesis of "Berserk" dates back to 1988 when Miura introduced a prototype of the series.
Its inaugural volume debuted on November 26, 1990, under the Jets Comics imprint by Hakusensha.

Following the release of three additional volumes, "Berserk" found its home in the pages of
Young Animal magazine in 1992, where it continues to captivate readers with its richly woven narrative and stunning artwork.
