What is Berserk?

"Berserk" (ベルセルク Beruseruku) is a renowned dark fantasy manga series crafted by the talented Kentaro Miura.
The genesis of "Berserk" dates back to 1988 when Miura introduced a prototype of the series.
Its inaugural volume debuted on November 26, 1990, under the Jets Comics imprint by Hakusensha.

Following the release of three additional volumes, "Berserk" found its home in the pages of
Young Animal magazine in 1992, where it continues to captivate readers with its richly woven narrative and stunning artwork.

"Berserk" is a manga series that resonates deeply with its audience through its compelling
storytelling and breathtaking artwork. Unflinching in its exploration of dark themes, it courageously confronts
challenging subject matter while offering a glimmer of hope amidst the darkness. The narrative highlights resilience
and determination in the face of adversity, seamlessly blending beauty and heartbreak
to leave a lasting impression and provoke profound contemplation.

"Berserk" thrives in its portrayal of complex
themes, eschewing simple solutions and delving into moral gray areas, challenging
conventional notions of right and wrong. A cornerstone of the manga world, "Berserk" is an unforgettable journey that sparks introspection and deep reflection.
